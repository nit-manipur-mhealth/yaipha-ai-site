About Yaipha AI

Yaipha AI is a technology startup focused on building safe, reliable and user-friendly digital health products. We work with doctors, clinics and patients to improve access to healthcare, especially in underserved regions.

Our team combines experience in software engineering, cloud infrastructure and healthcare workflows to build scalable, secure platforms.

Mediconex

Mediconex is a mobile application developed by Yaipha AI that helps patients connect with doctors for appointment booking and follow-up care.

Key Features

  • Book appointments with registered doctors and clinics.
  • Maintain basic health information and visit history.
  • Receive follow-up instructions and reminders.
  • Secure login and protected access to personal data.

Mediconex will be available on Android via the Google Play Store.
